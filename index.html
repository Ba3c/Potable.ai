We’re building intelligent knowledge assistants for organizations.

Our vision

We would like to build a platform for creating intelligent knowledge assistants for organizations in multiple domains. At present, organizations are not able to fully leverage LLMs due to many constraints. We aim to make LLMs fit for consumption by the organizations. LLMs are like power generated by power companies, and we are the power distribution company that delivers power to people’s homes. If LLMs are the river, we make the water potable and deliver to homes.
